Lyric Poster  
Collage 29.7 (h) x 21 (w) cm  

This poster adopts the lyrics “Ugly child, please don’t turn on the lights” from the song Ugly Child (醜八怪) by Jackie Xue. As the song intentionally restores the pain and cruelty of a social person living with a mask, this lyric indicates a child with an unpleasant appearance doesn’t want to face the world apparently but indeed represents the fear for letting others understand one’s soul. Since exposing the most vulnerable side to the world may destroy everything a person has, this song sings the struggle for living in authenticity.
