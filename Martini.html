01

Challenge of Martini

MARTINI is a truly global brand with an iconic logo, however it has been ‘missing in action’ for over 15 years and lost touch with a new generation of consumers. Awareness is high, but they are low on their target audience’s consideration list. MARTINI is not relevant to them and thought of as something their parents and grandparents drank.

02

​Purpose of advertising campaign

Create a brand experience that celebrates MARTINI’s campaign idea Let’s Make Time beyond traditional advertising. This campaign focus on the life the power and importance of spending quality time with friends using MARTINI. Our target audience are ‘Exuberants’: happy, social, high energy people who love to have fun. They enjoy meeting up with their friends for a drink to escape, to laugh, to share and to open up with people they are closest to. But the world they are living in is busy and complex. Although they value friendship, they often prioritise other things over meeting up with friends.

03

"NewTro"

Considering MARTINI's long history and an ageing consumer profile and lacks relevance with younger audiences. We implemented a new art direction for MARTINI to be "New and Retro", which has impression of being classic and elegant with contemporary and modern trends.

04

​Content of campaign

​There are 4 core aspect for our campaign, Including photo renovation of different timeslot for aperitivo, #LetsMakeTime campaign on Instagram, collaboration of drinks with public friendly brands, eg. Kit-Kat and Chupa Chups, and Pop-up booths in big events.
