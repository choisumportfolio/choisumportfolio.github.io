Cirkularity

Exhibition and event planning

CirKularity is an exhibition co-organized by K11 and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University that aims to promote sustainability through art and innovation. To be in line with K11, tenants from the fashion, cosmetic, and food & beverages industries show their support by donating materials to K11 for holding this exhibition. To ensure sustainability can be established in long term, CirKular’s award and K11’s eco-material library connect with tenants and visitors to form a new circularity system in K11.

CirKularity 是由K11與香港理工大學聯合主辦的展覽，旨在透過藝術和創新設計向大眾宣揚可持續發展的理念。部份時裝、美容和餐飲等行業的商戶以捐贈物資等行動來表達對於K11的品牌 理念和這次活動的支持。透過設立CirKular年度獎和K11環保物料圖書館，使K11作為商戶和客戶之間的橋樑，令可持續發展的理念得以落實，成為一個全新的商業模式。

Guest Journey

01

Corridor

Welcome visitors to the exhibition with a corridor lined with mirrors and the names of brands that took part in the event.

02

Introduction

Introduce the visitors about the exhibition of Cirkularity

03

Fashion Zone: The End of Seasonality
  

Explain the present state of textile waste in Hong Kong, how businesses are addressing the issue, and K11's support for companies who use sustainable materials.

04

Cosmetic Zone: Beauty and the Waste

To address the issue of cosmetic packaging waste, mixed materials should be replaced with refillable and mono packaging, K11 should demonstrate and lead the sustainable cosmetic trend, and potential tenants (who share the same vision) should be invited to join the K11 family.

05

F&B Zone: The Leftover

Showing the excessive garbage that humans produce while raising awareness on how to reduce waste by just consuming what you need. This demonstrates K11's social responsibility and promotes trash reduction, as commercial and industrial sources produce the majority of food waste.

06

The Hub

Material Lab collects all materials donated by brands and tenants and organizes them into a library. Visitors can use augmented reality to see the process from raw material to environmentally friendly material.

07

Message wall & The Shop

Visitors can make comments and purchase the tenants' sustainable items. K11 can collect feedback from visitors and promote environmentally friendly items to the general audience.
