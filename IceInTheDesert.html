01

Problems of current packaging

Ice cream is a sweet dessert that can recall people’s happiness and joyfulness ,it must be associated with positive beliefs and thoughts. However, it will easily melt while people are eating or taking to outdoor. Also the current packaging (paper) available in the supermarket and convenient store are not easy to handle. This project is aim to create an ice cream packaging that can keep cool, portable and sustainable. It can be hand-carried to outdoor activities, such as enjoy eating ice cream during picnic or on the beach ,as it can sustain in the hot atmosphere.

02

Concept

The main idea of the ice cream packaging adopts the natural phenomenon — Penitente, which snows falling on the desert and sublime to spike ice. And it will stays on the desert for the whole summer times. Desert which is at high attitude and under cold and dry conditions in absence of any liquid melt leads to the formation of Penitente. I use the idea of Penitente as a symbol for my ice cream packaging. As even in extreme environment of the hot desert, our ice cream can still survive anywhere.

03

Packaging specification

As the cups are double layered and the bottom has a hole to insert the ice-cold gel into the cup. So the cup can keep the ice cream cool for more than 8 hours in outdoor. The surface of the layer inside is curved, as curve surface can let customers eat ice cream with spoon smoothly without missing any one bite. Customers can hold the box through the handle. It has a mechanism in the bottom for customers to take the base ice cream first. This can lower the contact with the hot air of other uneaten ice creams on top.

04

Shape of cup

Each cup have different patterns and texture that represents a stage in penetente formation, they can combine to each others base and cover.
