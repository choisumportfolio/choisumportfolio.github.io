[SUR] NATUREL 

High jewlery event
Rosewood Hotels

A luxury jewellery exhibition events for Cartier pitched by Creative Agency Lore Ltd. It aims to transform the theme of Source Nature, Instinctive Nature and Imagined Nature of jewellery collections into experiences for guests.

Art Direction:  Andrew McWalters
3D Rendering:   Sherman Pang

Theme

[SUR]Naturel is the Cartier’s new High Jewellery collection, nature reveals a creative path and links figuration with abstraction. On one side, water, plants and animals. On the other, creations styling this primal energy into various details. A collection truer than nature, to go beyond what is real and reinvent it.
